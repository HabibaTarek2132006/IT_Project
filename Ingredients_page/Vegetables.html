Moussaka

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant - 2 large (sliced & fried or baked)
  • Ground beef - 300g
  • Onion - 1 (chopped)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves (minced)
  • Tomato sauce - 2 cups
  • Tomato paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green pepper - 1 (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin - 1 tsp
  • Oil - for frying or baking
