Beef Recipes

Beef Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced beef - 500g
  • Tomato sauce - 2 cups
  • Onion - 1 (chopped)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Bechamel sauce - 2 cups
  • Cheese (mozzarella) - 1 cup
  • Salt & pepper - to taste
  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp
