Beef Recipes

Beef Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced chicken or beef - 500g
  • Tomato sauce - 2 cups
  • Onion - 1 (chopped)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves
  • Bechamel sauce - 2 cups
  • Cheese (mozzarella) - 1 cup
  • Salt & pepper - to taste
  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp

Lamb Rogan Josh

Ingredients:

  • Lamb (or beef) - 500g (cut pieces)
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1 large (sliced)
  • Garlic - 4 cloves (minced)
  • Ginger - 1 tsp (grated)
  • Tomato puree - 1 cup
  • Oil or ghee - 2 tbsp
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1/2 tsp
  • Chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp

Lamb Rogan Josh

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast (cut into cubes) - 500g
  • Yogurt - 3 tbsp
  • Lemon juice - 1 tbsp
  • Garlic - 3 cloves (minced)
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Paprika - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Thyme or oregano - 1 tsp
  • Optional: Cumin - 1/2 tsp (for extra flavor)
