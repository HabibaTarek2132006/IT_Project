🍗 Chicken Recipes

Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine pasta - 250g
  • Chicken breast - 2 pieces (sliced)
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Garlic - 2 cloves (minced)
  • Heavy cream - 1 cup
  • Parmesan cheese - 1/2 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken (breast or thigh) - 500g
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Tomato puree - 1 cup
  • Heavy cream - 1/2 cup
  • Garlic - 3 cloves (minced)
  • Ginger - 1 tsp (grated)
  • Yogurt - 1 tbsp (for marination)
  • Paprika - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp

Chicken Shish Tawook

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast (cut into cubes) - 500g
  • Yogurt - 3 tbsp
  • Lemon juice - 1 tbsp
  • Garlic - 3 cloves (minced)
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Paprika - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Thyme or oregano - 1 tsp

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast - 500g (cubed)
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Lemon juice - 2 tbsp
  • Garam masala - 2 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1 tsp
  • Paprika - 1 tsp
  • Garlic - 3 cloves (minced)
  • Onion - 1 medium (chopped)
  • Tomato sauce - 1 cup
  • Heavy cream - 1/2 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp
