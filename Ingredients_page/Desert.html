Deserts Recipes

Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • Ladyfinger biscuits (Savoiardi)
  • Mascarpone cheese - 250g
  • Eggs - 2 (yolks only)
  • Sugar - 3 tbsp
  • Strong coffee - 1 cup (cold)
  • Cocoa powder - for topping
  • Vanilla extract - 1 tsp (optional)

Jalebi

Ingredients:

  • Flour - 1 cup
  • Corn flour - 2 tbsp
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar syrup
  • Saffron or food color
  • Oil for frying
